# Table 1: Demographics and Outcomes by Sepsis Definition

## Overview

Table 1 compares demographics, comorbidities, acuity, ICU utilization, life-support use, organ-failure patterns, timing, outcomes, and microbiology across **6 column groups** defined by sepsis-definition criteria. It is generated by `Code/02_table1.py` (a Marimo notebook) and exported as CSV.

### Column groups

| # | Group | Filter |
|---|-------|--------|
| 1 | **Total** | All hospitalizations in the cohort |
| 2 | **No Presumed Infection** | `presumed_infection == 0` |
| 3 | **Presumed Infection** | `presumed_infection == 1` |
| 4 | **ASE with Lactate** | `sepsis == 1` (meets ASE using all 6 CDC organ-dysfunction criteria, lactate included) |
| 5 | **ASE without Lactate** | `sepsis_wo_lactate == 1` (meets ASE using 5 criteria only; lactate cannot count) |
| 6 | **Lactate-only ASE** | `sepsis == 1` AND lactate is the **sole** organ dysfunction (all other 5 organ-failure timestamps are null) |

> **Groups are NOT mutually exclusive.** A patient in "ASE without Lactate" is also in "ASE with Lactate" and "Presumed Infection."

## Dataframe construction

The analysis dataset (`analysis_df`) is assembled in Step 5 of the notebook (lines 464-692):

1. **Base cohort** (`cohort_df.parquet`) — one row per hospitalization. Built by `01_cohort.py` with inclusion criteria: age >= 18, ED admission, academic/community hospital (no LTACH), admitted 2018-2024.
2. **ASE results** (`ase_df`) — left-joined on `hospitalization_id`. Supplies `presumed_infection`, `sepsis`, `sepsis_wo_lactate`, organ-failure datetimes, `total_qad`, and ASE-onset criteria columns. Only the **first ASE episode** (or first blood culture for non-ASE patients) is kept.
3. **Derived tables** — each left-joined on `hospitalization_id`:
   - `icu_df` — ICU indicators and LOS from ADT table (`location_category == 'icu'`)
   - `crrt_df` — CRRT receipt from CrrtTherapy table
   - `resp_df` — respiratory support (IMV, NIPPV, HFNO) from RespiratorySupport table
   - `vaso_df` — vasopressor receipt from MedicationAdminContinuous (`med_group == 'vasoactives'`)
   - `micro_df` — positive blood-culture count from MicrobiologyCulture (`fluid_category == 'blood_buffy'`)
   - `sofa_df` — max SOFA score per hospitalization (Polars-based computation via `compute_sofa_polars`)
   - `cci_df` — Charlson Comorbidity Index from HospitalDiagnosis (via `clifpy.utils.calculate_cci`)
   - `first_vital_df` — earliest vital-sign timestamp per hospitalization
   - `index_bc_df` — index blood-culture positivity and culture counts in a +/-2-day window

4. **Post-merge derivations** (lines 529-691):
   - Organ-failure indicator booleans (`had_aki`, `had_hyperbili`, etc.) from datetime presence
   - `organ_dysfunction_count` — sum of 6 boolean indicators
   - `first_organ_failure_dttm` — earliest of the 6 organ-failure datetimes
   - `time_to_organ_failure_hours` — admission to first organ failure
   - `time_to_second_organ_hours` — first to second organ failure (with lactate)
   - `lactate_to_second_organ_hours` — lactate to next organ failure (when lactate was first)
   - `non_lactate_to_second_organ_hours` — non-lactate first organ failure to next organ failure
   - `first_organ_dysfunction_w_lactate` / `first_organ_dysfunction_wo_lactate` — name of earliest organ dysfunction
   - `hospital_los_days` — (discharge - admission) in days
   - `time_to_bc_hours` — admission to blood-culture draw in hours
   - `in_hospital_death` — discharge category contains "expired" OR `death_dttm` <= `discharge_dttm`

## Group definitions (detail)

### Total
All hospitalizations meeting cohort criteria (adult, ED admission, academic/community hospital, 2018-2024).

### No Presumed Infection
`presumed_infection == 0`. The patient did not have a blood culture + qualifying antimicrobial day combination meeting the CDC ASE presumed-infection criteria.

### Presumed Infection
`presumed_infection == 1`. The patient had a blood culture drawn and received qualifying antimicrobials (QADs), meeting the CDC presumed-infection definition.

### ASE with Lactate
`sepsis == 1`. Meets full CDC Adult Sepsis Event criteria: presumed infection + at least 1 organ dysfunction from **6 criteria** (vasopressor, IMV, AKI, hyperbilirubinemia, thrombocytopenia, **elevated lactate**).

### ASE without Lactate
`sepsis_wo_lactate == 1`. Meets CDC ASE criteria using only **5 organ-dysfunction criteria** — elevated lactate is excluded. This is the stricter definition.

### Lactate-only ASE
`sepsis == 1` AND `lactate_dttm` is not null AND all 5 other organ-failure datetimes (`vasopressor_dttm`, `imv_dttm`, `aki_dttm`, `hyperbilirubinemia_dttm`, `thrombocytopenia_dttm`) are null. These patients qualified for ASE **only because of elevated lactate** — they would not meet ASE under the 5-criterion definition.

## Key notes

1. **Groups are not mutually exclusive.** Every "ASE without Lactate" patient is also "ASE with Lactate" and "Presumed Infection." The Lactate-only ASE group is a subset of "ASE with Lactate" that does not overlap with "ASE without Lactate."

2. **Two vasopressor columns exist.** `had_vasopressor` (Life Support section) = any vasoactive medication during the hospitalization from the MedicationAdminContinuous table. `had_vasopressor_ase` (Organ Failure section) = vasopressor use meeting CDC ASE organ-dysfunction timing criteria, from the ASE computation.

3. **Similarly, two IMV columns exist.** `had_imv` (Life Support) = any IMV during the stay. `had_imv_ase` (Organ Failure) = IMV meeting ASE criteria timing.

4. **Lactate-only logic.** The Lactate-only ASE group is identified by checking that `lactate_dttm` is present while all 5 other organ-failure datetimes are null. This isolates patients whose ASE status depends entirely on the lactate criterion.

5. **Index BC window.** The "index" blood culture is the BC from the ASE computation. Positivity is determined by matching microbiology results collected within +/-1 calendar day. The broader "window" for culture counts uses +/-2 days.

6. **QADs are N/A for non-infection groups.** Qualifying Antimicrobial Days only exist in the context of presumed infection, so Total and No Presumed Infection groups show N/A.

7. **ICU LOS is conditional.** ICU length of stay is only computed for patients who actually had an ICU stay (`had_icu == True`).

8. **In-hospital death** is determined by either the discharge category containing "expired" or a recorded `death_dttm` that falls on or before `discharge_dttm`.

9. **Organ failure timing** uses the 6 organ-dysfunction datetimes from the ASE computation (not from the broader CLIF tables). `time_to_organ_failure_hours` = earliest organ-failure datetime minus admission datetime.

10. **ICU type columns are dynamic.** The specific ICU-type columns (e.g., MICU, SICU) depend on what `location_type` values exist in the site's ADT data. They are auto-detected at runtime.

11. **Race and ethnicity categories are dynamic.** Raw race and ethnicity categories are taken from whatever unique values exist in the data, while the combined `race_ethnicity` column uses a fixed set of 6 categories.

12. **ASE is computed with `include_lactate=True`** in `01_cohort.py`, which produces both the lactate-inclusive (`sepsis`) and lactate-exclusive (`sepsis_wo_lactate`) flags in a single pass. The `apply_rit=True` flag enables relative increase in creatinine criteria for AKI.
